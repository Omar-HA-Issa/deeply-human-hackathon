## What you can do
### Core experience
- **Explore the world on a 3D globe** and select countries to quiz.
- **Answer curated quiz questions** that mix core facts with surprising insights.
- **Earn XP and points** for accuracy and speed.
- **Unlock countries progressively** by completing quizzes, following the map‚Äôs adjacency rules.

### Social play
- **Add friends and send challenges** in any country.
- **Compete in friend matches** and compare results.
- **Celebrate streaks and progress** through shared activity.

> Friend matches are designed for play without breaking progression rules‚Äîyou can challenge friends anywhere without unlocking countries you haven't earned yet.

---

## Gameplay loop
1. Choose an available country.
2. Take the quiz and submit answers.
3. Earn XP, points, and completion status.
4. Unlock neighboring countries as you progress.
5. Repeat and grow your world knowledge.
